Features

Everything you need to stay connected

Built for families who care about each other's journeys — not just the destination.

📡

Real-Time Flight Tracking

Watch flights move across the globe in real-time. See altitude, speed, and estimated arrival — updated every minute so you're never guessing.

✈️

Share with Family

Send a link, and everyone can follow the same flight. No app download required for viewers — just tap and track.
